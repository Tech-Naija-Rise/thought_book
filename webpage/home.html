Thought Book

Think Clearly. Write Privately.

Thought Book is a fast, offline-first note app for Windows. It gives you a private space to capture your thoughts — without cloud logins, or internet distractions.

Why Thought Book?

  • Offline & Private: Notes are saved locally, encrypted, and never sent online.
  • Instant Access: Opens in a second, even on basic systems.
  • Simple Protection: One-password lock and recovery code for your notes.
  • Clean Interface: Dark theme, sidebar navigation, auto-save.
  • Upgrade Anytime: Unlock unlimited notes and premium features easily.
